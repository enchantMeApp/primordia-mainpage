THE DISCOVERY

In 2024, satellite imagery revealed something impossible‚Äîa chain of uncharted islands in the South Pacific, shrouded by perpetual storm systems that had hidden them from modern detection for centuries. When the clouds finally parted, the world discovered the Primordia Archipelago.

Initial reconnaissance missions uncovered the truth: these weren't natural islands. Ancient containment facilities, built by an unknown civilization, had preserved ecosystems filled with creatures that should have been extinct for millions of years. Dinosaurs, megafauna, and species never catalogued by science roamed freely across these forgotten lands.

The discovery sparked a modern gold rush. Fortune seekers, scientists, poachers, and adventurers flocked to the archipelago, lured by promises of untold wealth from rare genetic samples, exotic resources, and the chance to tame living legends. Companies offered exorbitant sums for viable dinosaur DNA. Black markets exploded with demand for creature parts worth more than diamonds.

Ships can sail in, but cannot sail out. Aircraft experience complete system failures upon entry. Communication with the outside world is impossible.

Those who came seeking fortune found themselves trapped in a prehistoric nightmare. Now, thousands of survivors struggle to stay alive, establishing makeshift camps, learning to hunt, craft, and fight. Some have given up hope of escape and built new lives. Others desperately search for a way to break the barrier.

The islands are unforgiving. Resources are scarce. Territories are contested. Massive alpha predators rule their domains. And every day, more ships arrive, bringing fresh souls into the trap‚Äînew competition for dwindling supplies, or perhaps new allies in the fight for survival.

You arrived three days ago. Your supplies are running low. The jungle grows darker. Will you master this world, or become another casualty of Primordia?
